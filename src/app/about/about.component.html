GoTour
Contains tourist places available in the district of Wayanad,Kerala and The Nilgiris,Tamilnadu.This is a User-friendly website that can be accessed by every tourists from around the world. The website is useful for not only the major attractive places but also the unnoticed minor tourist attractions.Since the details of the tourist place is displayed, it reduces the risk of scamming .This website will be very useful for the development of tourist attraction in Wayanad and The Nilgiris it will act as an adequate tourist guide for the travellers.And there is also a suggestion page in this website that allows user to recommend hidden tourist places from both the districts of Wayanad and The Nilgiris.
